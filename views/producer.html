ibm.com/cloud-computing

This is my largest effort right now, which I'm leading as a producer, running a semi-agile team, consisting of a mix of IBMers and Havas folks. We're live at ibm.com/cloud-computing.

Last fall, we were under the gun to get a first iteration live in time for Gartner Symposium 2015, so we deployed in under 2 months. Now we've been working through strategy, and iterating design and IA. We're also running A/B tests through Optimizely! Look out for major improvements in Q2.

IBM PartnerWorld University

I ran a tight, semi-agile design team to revamp IBM's product education platform for their sales partners. This was also an effort where I ran point on client management. Our team was small and efficient on this project, and spending significant time working through challenges with my UX and visual designers was my favorite part.

Our designs were implemented, but unfortunately, by a different organization under a different scope (one of my gripes with the advertising industry), so I'm not entirely pleased with the end results. Instead, I'll post my teams comps which I can speak for :)

LoJack's biglojackarrow.com

In addition to serving as UX Designer on biglojackarrow.com, I also produced the digital offerings of the campaign, which included online banners, a microsite that served as a hub for the campaign as well as a contest entry point, and finally a service to enter by SMS.

People were driven to the site by the online banner ads, but more importantly from our street teams roaming LoJack's 4 biggest markets in cars with 6 foot flashing arrows on top, demonstrating LoJack's ability to locate vehicles.
